# Multiscale Agency Grounding Bundle v0.2

## Status

`PRESERVED_CANDIDATE_RESEARCH_BUNDLE`

This directory preserves the evidence-grounded working bibliography and supporting audit artifacts developed for the physics-inspired multiscale human/non-human agency research program.

The bundle is **not yet integrated into** `bibliography/**/*.bib`, the repository's canonical citation surface. The master file is canonical only for this bounded research project pending repository-level deduplication, tier mapping, transformation tagging, metadata normalization, local validation, and independent review.

## Epistemic boundary

The included sources ground:

- Michael Levin and colleagues' work on multiscale competency, basal cognition, morphogenesis, bioelectric coordination, and collective intelligence;
- source physics and mathematics used as inspiration for candidate agentic models;
- Alex Wissner-Gross and related future-state, causal-entropy, collective-motion, and human-machine works;
- explicit critiques and terminology distinctions needed to prevent concept collapse.

The sources do **not** independently validate the human-AI translations. Constructs such as the Multiscale Three-Agent Problem, Agentic Frustration, Agency Percolation, Renormalization of Agency, and Epistemic Metabolism remain candidate models requiring operational definitions, falsifiers, experiments, and adversarial review.
